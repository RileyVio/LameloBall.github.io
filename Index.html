LaMelo Ball

LaMelo Ball is a professional basketball player who plays as a point guard for the Charlotte Hornets in the NBA. He is known for his flashy passing, deep shooting range, and creative playstyle.

Born on August 22, 2001, he quickly became one of the most exciting young players in the league. He won NBA Rookie of the Year in 2021 and is known for his confidence and highlight plays.
